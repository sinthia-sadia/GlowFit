Standing Kickbacks

Activate and tone your glutes with this standing bodyweight move.

How to Do Standing Kickbacks

  1. Stand straight with hands on hips or holding a wall/chair for support.
  2. Shift your weight to one leg and slowly kick the opposite leg straight back.
  3. Squeeze your glutes at the top of the movement, keeping your back straight.
  4. Return your leg to the starting position and repeat. Switch sides after a set.

Reps & Sets

Perform 3 sets of 15 reps per leg. Rest for 30 seconds between sets.

Benefits

  • Targets the gluteus maximus for a lifted butt
  • Improves hip stability and balance
  • Great for activating muscles before heavier workouts
