Side Leg Circles

Tone your thighs and improve hip mobility with controlled leg circles.

How to Do Side Leg Circles

  1. Lie on one side with legs stacked and straight, supporting your head with your hand.
  2. Lift the top leg slightly and draw small circles with your toes in the air.
  3. Complete the reps in one direction, then reverse the circles.
  4. Keep your core engaged and hips stable throughout the movement.

Reps & Sets

Perform 15 circles forward and 15 backward per leg. Complete 3 sets total.

Benefits

  • Targets inner and outer thighs
  • Improves hip flexibility and control
  • Great for warm-ups or toning routines
