💪 Shoulder Stretch

Release shoulder tension and improve flexibility with this simple and effective stretch.

How to Perform

Stand or sit tall. Bring your right arm across your body at chest height. Use your left arm to gently pull the right arm closer to your chest, feeling a stretch in the shoulder. Hold for 20–30 seconds, then switch arms. Repeat 2–3 times per side.

Benefits

  • Relieves tightness in shoulders and upper back
  • Improves shoulder mobility and flexibility
  • Reduces risk of shoulder injuries
  • Helps with posture and alignment

Tips for Best Results

  • Keep your shoulders down and relaxed during the stretch
  • Don’t force your arm – stretch to a comfortable limit
  • Breathe deeply and steadily
  • Stretch daily for best results
