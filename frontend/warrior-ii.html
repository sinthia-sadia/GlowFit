🛡️ Warrior II

A strong and grounding pose that builds endurance and balance.

Overview

Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II) is a powerful standing pose that enhances stability, focus, and lower body strength.

🧘 Steps to Perform Warrior II

  1. Start in a standing position, step one foot back wide.
  2. Front foot points forward, back foot turns slightly inward.
  3. Bend your front knee so it's above your ankle.
  4. Stretch arms out parallel to the floor, gaze over front fingers.
  5. Hold the pose for several breaths, then switch sides.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • Yoga mat for stability
  • Comfortable clothing

💪 Benefits

  • Strengthens legs, hips, and core
  • Improves balance and focus
  • Enhances stamina and endurance
  • Expands chest and lungs

✅ Tips

  • Keep shoulders relaxed and core engaged
  • Press firmly into both feet for stability
  • Lengthen through the crown of your head
  • Keep front thigh parallel to the ground if possible

🔥 Estimated Calories Burned

You may burn around 35–60 calories per 10 minutes depending on intensity and body weight.
