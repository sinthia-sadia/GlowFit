🧘 Yoga Workout

Relax and strengthen your body with these yoga flows designed to improve flexibility, balance, and mindfulness.

Sun Salutation

A sequence of poses to warm up the body and mind.

Downward Dog

Stretches your hamstrings and calves while strengthening your arms and shoulders.

Warrior II

Builds strength and stability in your legs and core.

🧰 Equipment Needed

  • Yoga mat (non-slip for safety)
  • Yoga blocks (for support in flexibility poses)
  • Strap or belt (for deep stretching)
  • Water bottle (to stay hydrated)

🌟 Benefits

  • Improves flexibility and balance
  • Builds core strength and posture
  • Reduces stress and promotes mindfulness
  • Supports joint and muscle health
  • Boosts circulation and energy levels

💡 Tips

  • Warm up before starting the routine
  • Focus on deep, steady breathing
  • Use props for comfort and better form
  • Stay consistent and patient with progress
  • Practice in a quiet, clean space
