🏋️ Jump Squats

Explosive movement to build power and tone your lower body

How to Do Jump Squats

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, perform a squat, then jump up explosively. Land softly and go directly into the next squat.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • Comfortable shoes
  • Non-slip mat or flooring

💡 Benefits

  • Boosts explosive power and strength
  • Tones glutes, quads, and calves
  • Elevates heart rate for cardio
  • Improves lower body endurance

✅ Tips

  • Land softly on your feet to reduce impact
  • Keep your chest lifted and back straight
  • Use your arms for momentum
  • Engage your core throughout the movement
