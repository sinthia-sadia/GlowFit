🍑 Butt Sculpt Workout

18 minutes • 8 workouts to tone and strengthen your glutes

🍑 Glute Bridges

30 seconds of bridges to activate your glutes and hamstrings.

🍑 Donkey Kicks

40 seconds of controlled donkey kicks to sculpt your butt.

🍑 Fire Hydrants

40 seconds targeting hip mobility and glute strength.

🍑 Jump Squats

30 seconds of explosive jump squats to build power.

🍑 Side-Lying Leg Lifts

40 seconds focused on outer glute and hip muscles.

🍑 Hip Thrusts

45 seconds to maximize glute strength and endurance.

🍑 Wall Sit

1 minute of isometric hold to build lower body strength.

🏋️‍♀️ Equipment Needed

  • Comfortable workout clothes and shoes
  • Yoga mat or soft surface for floor exercises
  • Resistance bands or dumbbells (optional for added intensity)

💡 Benefits

  • Tones and sculpts glute muscles
  • Improves hip stability and balance
  • Increases lower body strength
  • Enhances athletic performance

✅ Tips for Best Results

  • Focus on controlled movements and form
  • Engage your core to protect your back
  • Take breaks if needed and stay hydrated
  • Gradually increase reps or resistance
  • Finish with stretches for hips and thighs
