Squats

Build strength and tone your legs and glutes with squats.

How to do Squats

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Keep your chest up and back straight.
  3. Lower your body by bending your knees and pushing your hips back.
  4. Go down until your thighs are parallel to the floor.
  5. Press through your heels to stand back up.

Reps & Sets

Do 3 sets of 15 reps, resting 30 seconds between sets.

Benefits

  • Strengthens legs and glutes
  • Improves balance and flexibility
  • Supports better posture
