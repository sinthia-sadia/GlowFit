🔥 Fire Hydrants

Strengthen and tone your glutes and hips with this targeted move

How to Do Fire Hydrants

Begin on all fours, hands under shoulders, knees under hips. Keeping your knee bent, lift one leg out to the side until your thigh is parallel to the ground, then lower. Avoid twisting your hips; keep them square to the floor.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • Exercise mat
  • Resistance band (optional)

💡 Benefits

  • Targets glutes and hip abductors
  • Improves hip mobility
  • Helps with posture and lower back strength

✅ Tips for Best Results

  • Keep your core engaged to avoid lower back strain
  • Don’t swing your leg — move slowly and with control
  • Focus on muscle activation rather than speed
