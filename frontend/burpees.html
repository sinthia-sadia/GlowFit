🔥 Burpees

A full-body exercise that builds cardio endurance and strength

How to Do Burpees

Start standing, then drop into a squat, place your hands on the floor, kick your feet back into a plank position, perform a push-up, jump your feet back toward your hands, and explosively jump into the air. Repeat for the desired number of reps or time.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • Comfortable workout clothes
  • Yoga mat (optional for floor comfort)

💡 Benefits

  • Boosts cardiovascular fitness
  • Improves strength and endurance
  • Burns calories and fat
  • Full-body workout engaging multiple muscle groups

✅ Tips for Best Results

  • Keep your core tight throughout
  • Land softly to reduce joint impact
  • Modify by skipping push-ups or jumps if needed
  • Maintain steady breathing
  • Start slow, increase speed as you improve
