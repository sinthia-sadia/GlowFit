🏃 Mountain Climbers

High-intensity core and cardio exercise to build endurance and agility

How to Do Mountain Climbers

Start in a high plank position. Drive one knee towards your chest, then switch legs quickly, like running in place while keeping your hands planted. Engage your core and move at a steady pace for 30–60 seconds.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • Comfortable workout clothes
  • Yoga mat (optional)
  • Water bottle nearby

💡 Benefits

  • Burns calories fast
  • Tones core and abs
  • Improves cardiovascular endurance
  • Enhances agility and coordination

✅ Tips

  • Keep your back flat and core tight
  • Move quickly but maintain form
  • Don't let your hips sag or rise too high
  • Breathe evenly throughout
