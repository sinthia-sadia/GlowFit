3 sets of 20 reps. Effective for targeting upper abdominal muscles.

How to Do Crunches

Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Place your hands behind your head or crossed on your chest. Engage your core and lift your upper body towards your knees, then slowly lower back down.

Tips for Best Results

  • Keep your neck relaxed to avoid strain.
  • Use controlled movements and avoid jerking.
  • Breathe out when lifting, breathe in when lowering.

Equipment

  • Exercise mat (optional for comfort)
  • Comfortable workout clothes

Benefits

  • Strengthens the upper abdominal muscles
  • Improves core stability and posture
  • Enhances overall body balance
  • Supports better athletic performance
