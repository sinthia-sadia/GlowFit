🏃‍♂️ Jumping Jacks

A full-body warm-up exercise to increase your heart rate and blood flow.

How to Do Jumping Jacks

Jumping jacks are a classic cardio exercise that improves endurance, coordination, and overall fitness.

  1. Stand upright with your legs together and arms at your sides.
  2. Jump up, spreading your legs shoulder-width apart while raising your arms overhead.
  3. Jump again, returning your arms to your sides and your legs together.
  4. Repeat this motion smoothly for the recommended duration.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • Comfortable workout clothes and shoes
  • Optional: Yoga mat for joint support

💪 Benefits

  • Improves cardiovascular endurance
  • Enhances coordination and balance
  • Burns calories and supports weight loss
  • Engages multiple muscle groups for a full-body workout

✅ Tips

  • Maintain an upright posture throughout
  • Land softly on your feet to protect your joints
  • Start at a moderate pace and increase speed gradually
  • Breathe steadily and deeply
  • Warm up before starting and cool down after

🔥 Estimated Calories Burned

Approximately 50–100 calories per 10 minutes depending on intensity and body weight.
