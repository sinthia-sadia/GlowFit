🏃‍♂️ High Knees

An intense cardio move to get your heart pumping and legs burning

How to Do High Knees

Stand tall and jog in place, lifting your knees as high as possible toward your chest. Pump your arms as if sprinting and move quickly for 30–60 seconds.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • Comfortable shoes and workout clothes
  • Non-slip surface or mat

💡 Benefits

  • Increases heart rate quickly
  • Strengthens legs and core
  • Boosts endurance and coordination
  • Improves running performance

✅ Tips

  • Keep your back straight and chest up
  • Move quickly but with control
  • Land softly to reduce impact
  • Use your arms to maintain rhythm
