Lateral Lunges

Strengthen your inner and outer thighs while improving balance and flexibility.

How to Do Lateral Lunges

  1. Stand tall with your feet hip-width apart and hands at your sides or in front of your chest.
  2. Take a big step to the right, bending your right knee and pushing your hips back as you lower into a lunge.
  3. Keep your left leg straight and your chest lifted.
  4. Push off the right foot to return to the starting position.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
