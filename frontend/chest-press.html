🏋️ Chest Press

Lift and firm your chest with this effective dumbbell exercise

📝 Description

The Chest Press is a powerful upper body exercise that targets your pectoral muscles, triceps, and shoulders. Using dumbbells or a barbell, you press weights upward while lying on a bench or mat, which helps to lift and tone your chest.

📋 How to Perform

  1. Lie on your back on a bench or mat, knees bent and feet flat on the ground.
  2. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with elbows bent and arms out to your sides.
  3. Exhale as you press the dumbbells straight up over your chest.
  4. Pause briefly at the top, then slowly lower the weights back down.
  5. Repeat for 3 sets of 12 reps.

✅ Tips

  • Keep your wrists straight and stable during the press.
  • Engage your core to maintain balance and form.
  • Don't lock your elbows at the top of the press.
  • Start with light weights and increase gradually.
