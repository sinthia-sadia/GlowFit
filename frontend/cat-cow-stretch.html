Gently mobilize your spine and improve flexibility with the Cat-Cow stretch.

How to Perform

Begin on your hands and knees in a tabletop position, with your wrists directly under your shoulders and your knees under your hips. Inhale as you drop your belly towards the mat, lifting your head and tailbone towards the ceiling (Cow Pose). Exhale as you round your spine towards the ceiling, tucking your chin to your chest and drawing your navel toward your spine (Cat Pose). Continue flowing between Cow and Cat poses for 5‚Äì10 breaths, moving with your breath.

Benefits

  • Improves spine flexibility and posture
  • Stimulates abdominal organs, aiding digestion
  • Reduces stress and calms the mind
  • Supports better blood circulation
  • Eases neck and back tension

Tips for Best Results

  • Move slowly and gently to avoid injury
  • Focus on synchronizing your breath with movement
  • Keep your shoulders relaxed and away from your ears
  • Practice daily to maintain spinal health
