Gently stretch and relax your neck muscles to relieve tension and improve flexibility.

How to Perform

Sit or stand up straight with your shoulders relaxed. Slowly tilt your head toward your right shoulder until you feel a gentle stretch on the left side of your neck. Hold this position for 20 seconds, breathing deeply and steadily. Return your head to the center, then repeat on the left side. Repeat 2-3 times per side.

Benefits

  • Relieves neck tension and stiffness
  • Improves neck flexibility and range of motion
  • Reduces headaches caused by neck strain
  • Promotes better posture

Tips for Best Results

  • Move slowly and gently to avoid injury
  • Focus on relaxing your shoulders while stretching
  • Maintain steady breathing throughout the stretch
  • Perform daily or as needed to relieve tension
