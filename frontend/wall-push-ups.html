Wall Push-Ups are a simple and effective bodyweight exercise for beginners. They help tone the chest, shoulders, and arms while being easy on the joints. Perfect for those starting out or recovering from injury.

ğŸ“‹ How to Perform

  1. Stand facing a wall, arms length away, feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Place your hands flat on the wall at chest level, slightly wider than your shoulders.
  3. Bend your elbows slowly and lean toward the wall until your face is close to it.
  4. Push yourself back to the starting position by straightening your arms.
  5. Repeat for 3 sets of 20 reps.

  • Engage your core to maintain balance and stability.
  • Avoid locking your elbows at the top of the movement.
  • Step farther from the wall to increase intensity.
