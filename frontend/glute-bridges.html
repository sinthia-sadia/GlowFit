🍑 Glute Bridges

Strengthen your glutes and improve hip mobility with this classic move

How to Do Glute Bridges

Lie on your back with knees bent, feet flat on the floor, and arms by your sides. Push through your heels to lift your hips until your body forms a straight line from shoulders to knees. Squeeze your glutes at the top and lower slowly.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • Exercise mat or soft surface
  • Resistance band or dumbbell (optional)

💡 Benefits

  • Targets and strengthens the glutes
  • Improves posture and hip mobility
  • Reduces lower back pain
  • Enhances core and hamstring strength

✅ Tips for Best Results

  • Drive through your heels, not your toes
  • Keep your core engaged
  • Don’t overextend your lower back
  • Hold and squeeze at the top of each rep
