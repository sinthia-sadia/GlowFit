🍑 Wall Sit

An isometric exercise to build endurance in your thighs and glutes

How to Do Wall Sit

Stand with your back flat against a wall and feet shoulder-width apart. Slide your back down the wall until your thighs are parallel to the floor, knees directly above ankles. Hold this position, keeping your core engaged.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • Wall with enough space
  • Comfortable shoes
  • Optional: Yoga mat for comfort

💡 Benefits

  • Strengthens quadriceps, glutes, and calves
  • Improves muscular endurance
  • Supports knee stability
  • Enhances core strength

✅ Tips for Best Results

  • Keep your back flat against the wall
  • Do not let your knees go past your toes
  • Engage your core and breathe steadily
  • Hold for as long as you can, gradually increasing time
  • Use a yoga mat for cushioning if needed
