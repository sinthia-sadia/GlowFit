Chest Workout

Tone and strengthen your chest muscles with these exercises

Push-Ups

3 sets of 15 reps. Great for strengthening the chest and arms. Can modify on knees if needed.

Chest Press

3 sets of 12 reps using dumbbells. Lie on your back and push weights upward. Helps lift and firm the chest.

Chest Fly

3 sets of 12 reps with dumbbells. Open arms wide and squeeze back together to work chest muscles.
