🏃‍♂️ Quad Stretch

Improve mobility and reduce tightness in your thighs with this simple stretch.

How to Perform

Stand tall with your feet hip-width apart. Bend your right knee and bring your heel toward your buttocks. Grab your ankle with your right hand and gently pull to deepen the stretch. Keep your knees close together and your back straight. Hold for 20–30 seconds, then switch legs. Repeat 2–3 times per side.

Benefits

  • Increases flexibility in quadriceps
  • Improves range of motion in knees and hips
  • Reduces risk of injury during workouts
  • Relieves tightness and soreness after exercise

Tips for Best Results

  • Hold onto a wall or chair for balance if needed
  • Keep your hips aligned and avoid leaning forward
  • Stretch after warming up your muscles
  • Don’t pull too hard — aim for a gentle stretch
