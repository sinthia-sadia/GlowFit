🏋️ Chest Fly

Expand and sculpt your chest with this controlled dumbbell movement

📝 Description

The Chest Fly is a focused strength exercise that stretches and works the chest muscles using a wide arc motion. It helps tone, shape, and firm the chest by targeting the pectorals without involving the triceps as much as presses.

📋 How to Perform

  1. Lie flat on a bench or mat with a dumbbell in each hand, arms extended above your chest.
  2. Keep a slight bend in your elbows and slowly lower your arms out to the sides.
  3. Lower until your arms are level with your chest and you feel a stretch in your pecs.
  4. Squeeze your chest and bring the dumbbells back up in a hugging motion.
  5. Repeat for 3 sets of 12 reps.

✅ Tips

  • Use light to moderate dumbbells to avoid shoulder strain.
  • Maintain controlled motion – don’t let the weights drop too quickly.
  • Focus on squeezing your chest muscles at the top of the movement.
  • Avoid locking out your elbows or overextending your arms.
