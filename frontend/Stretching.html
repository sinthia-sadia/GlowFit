Improve flexibility and relax muscles with these full body stretches

Neck Stretch

Gently tilt your head to one side and hold for 20 seconds. Repeat on the other side. Relieves tension in the neck muscles.

Shoulder Stretch

Bring one arm across your chest and hold it with the opposite arm for 20-30 seconds. Loosens shoulder tightness.

Cat-Cow Stretch

On all fours, alternate arching your back upward (cat) and dipping it down (cow) for 1-2 minutes. Increases spine flexibility.

Seated Forward Bend

Sit with legs extended, reach forward towards your toes and hold for 30 seconds. Stretches hamstrings and lower back.

Quad Stretch

Standing, pull one foot behind you toward your buttocks and hold for 20-30 seconds. Stretch your front thigh muscles.
