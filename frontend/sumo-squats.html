Sumo Squats

Target your inner thighs and glutes with this powerful lower-body move.

How to Do Sumo Squats

  1. Stand with your feet wider than shoulder-width apart, toes pointed slightly outward.
  2. Keep your chest up and core engaged.
  3. Lower your body by bending your knees and pushing your hips back, keeping your back straight.
  4. Descend until your thighs are parallel to the floor.
  5. Push through your heels to return to the starting position.
