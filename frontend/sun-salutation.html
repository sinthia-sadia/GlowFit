How to Do Sun Salutation

Sun Salutation (Surya Namaskar) is a sequence of yoga poses performed in a flow to warm up the body and connect breath with movement.

  1. Start in Mountain Pose (Tadasana) standing tall.
  2. Inhale and raise your arms overhead (Urdhva Hastasana).
  3. Exhale and bend forward into Forward Fold (Uttanasana).
  4. Inhale to a half lift (Ardha Uttanasana), lengthening your spine.
  5. Exhale and step back into Plank Pose.
  6. Lower down slowly into Chaturanga Dandasana.
  7. Inhale into Upward Facing Dog or Cobra Pose.
  8. Exhale to Downward Facing Dog.
  9. Inhale, step forward to Half Lift.
  10. Exhale, fold forward.
  11. Inhale, sweep your arms overhead.
  12. Exhale, return to Mountain Pose.
