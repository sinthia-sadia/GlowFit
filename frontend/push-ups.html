💪 Push-Ups

3 sets of 10-15 reps. Works both triceps and biceps along with shoulders and chest.

Exercise Description

Start in a plank position with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor, then push back up to the starting position.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • None – bodyweight exercise
  • Comfortable workout clothes

💪 Benefits

  • Strengthens chest, shoulders, triceps, and core
  • Improves upper body endurance and power
  • Enhances posture and functional strength
  • Can be done anywhere without equipment

✅ Tips

  • Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels
  • Engage your core and avoid sagging hips
  • Lower yourself slowly with control
  • Breathe in while lowering, breathe out while pushing up
  • Modify by doing on knees if needed
