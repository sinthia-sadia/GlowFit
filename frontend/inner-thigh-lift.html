Inner Thigh Lift

Target and tone your inner thighs with controlled, focused leg lifts.

How to Do Inner Thigh Lifts

  1. Lie on your side with your bottom leg extended and top leg bent over it.
  2. Keep your core engaged and lift your bottom leg upward.
  3. Pause briefly at the top, then lower with control.
  4. Repeat for one side, then switch to the other leg.

Reps & Sets

Perform 3 sets of 15 reps per leg. Rest 30 seconds between sides.

Benefits

  • Targets inner thigh muscles (adductors)
  • Improves hip stability and control
  • Increases leg flexibility and tone
