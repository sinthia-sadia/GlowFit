3 sets of 20 circles in each direction. Great for warming up shoulders and improving flexibility.

How to Do Arm Circles

Stand tall with your feet shoulder-width apart. Extend your arms straight out to the sides at shoulder height. Slowly make small circles forward with your arms, gradually increasing the size of the circles. After completing the set, reverse the direction.

This exercise warms up your shoulders, improves range of motion, and increases blood flow to the muscles.

Benefits of Arm Circles

  • Improves shoulder mobility and flexibility
  • Enhances blood circulation in the arms and shoulders
  • Helps reduce muscle stiffness and tension
  • Prepares your upper body for more intense workouts

Equipment Needed

  • No equipment needed ‚Äî just your body!
  • Optional: light dumbbells for added resistance

Tips for Proper Form

  • Keep your arms fully extended but not locked
  • Maintain a controlled, steady motion
  • Start with small circles and gradually increase size
  • Breathe normally throughout the exercise
