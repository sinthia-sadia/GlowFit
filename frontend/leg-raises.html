Leg Raises

3 sets of 15 reps. Great for lower abs and hip flexors.

How to do Leg Raises

Lie flat on your back with legs straight. Slowly lift your legs until they are perpendicular to the floor, then lower them back down without touching the floor. Keep your core tight throughout the movement.

  • Keep your lower back pressed to the floor.
  • Move slowly to maximize muscle engagement.
  • Breathe steadily and avoid holding your breath.

Equipment

  • Exercise mat for comfort
  • Comfortable workout attire

Benefits

  • Strengthens lower abdominal muscles
  • Improves hip flexor flexibility and strength
  • Enhances core stability and posture
  • Supports better balance and athletic performance
