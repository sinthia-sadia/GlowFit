🔥 Belly Burn Workout

Strengthen and tone your core with these belly-focused exercises

Crunches

3 sets of 20 reps. Effective for targeting upper abdominal muscles.

Leg Raises

3 sets of 15 reps. Great for lower abs and hip flexors.

Mountain Climbers

3 sets of 30 seconds. A full-body cardio move that also targets the core.

Plank

Hold for 1 minute. Builds core endurance and stability.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • Comfortable workout clothes and shoes
  • Yoga mat or soft surface
  • Light dumbbells or resistance bands (optional)

💪 Benefits

  • Strengthens core muscles and tones belly area
  • Improves posture and balance
  • Enhances metabolic rate and calorie burn
  • Supports overall functional fitness

✅ Tips

  • Warm up well before starting exercises
  • Focus on controlled, steady movements
  • Keep your core engaged throughout each move
  • Maintain consistent breathing
  • Rest briefly between sets for recovery
