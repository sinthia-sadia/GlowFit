Plank

Engage your core and build endurance with this essential stabilization move.

How to Do a Plank

  1. Start on your hands and knees, then extend your legs behind you.
  2. Support your body on your forearms and toes.
  3. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  4. Engage your core and hold the position without sagging or arching your back.

Hold Duration

Hold the plank for 45 seconds. Increase duration as you build strength.

Benefits

  • Strengthens the core, shoulders, and back
  • Improves posture and balance
  • Enhances body awareness and stability
