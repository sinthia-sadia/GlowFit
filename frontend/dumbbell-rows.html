Dumbbell Rows

Strengthen your upper back and improve posture with this effective pulling exercise.

How to Do Dumbbell Rows

  1. Place one knee and one hand on a bench, keeping your back flat.
  2. Hold a dumbbell in the opposite hand with your arm extended downward.
  3. Pull the dumbbell toward your waist, squeezing your shoulder blade.
  4. Lower the weight with control and repeat for each arm.

Benefits

  • Builds upper back, lats, and biceps
  • Improves posture and shoulder stability
  • Enhances pulling strength for compound lifts
