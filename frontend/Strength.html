🏋️‍♀️ Strength Circuit Workout

Build muscle and endurance with this full-body strength circuit routine

Push-Ups

12 reps — Builds upper body strength targeting chest, shoulders, and triceps. Maintain a straight body line throughout.

Dumbbell Rows

12 reps per arm — Targets back muscles and improves posture. Use controlled movements and avoid swinging.

Lunges

10 reps per leg — Strengthens legs and improves balance. Step forward with control and keep your knees aligned.

Benefits

  • Improves muscular strength and endurance
  • Enhances cardiovascular fitness
  • Supports fat loss and lean muscle gain

Tips

  • Focus on form over speed to avoid injury
  • Breathe steadily throughout each exercise
  • Adjust weights and reps according to your fitness level
