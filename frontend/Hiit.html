High-Intensity Interval Training to boost metabolism and burn fat fast

Jumping Jacks

Perform for 45 seconds, then rest for 15 seconds. Great full-body warm-up to get your heart rate up.

Burpees

30 seconds high-intensity burpees followed by 30 seconds rest. Excellent for building cardio and full-body strength.

High Knees

45 seconds sprinting in place with knees up, rest 15 seconds. Improves speed and cardiovascular fitness.

Squat Jumps

30 seconds explosive squat jumps followed by 30 seconds rest. Builds leg power and burns calories.
