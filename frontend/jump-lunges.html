Jump Lunges

Explosive lower body move to build strength, power, and endurance.

How to Do Jump Lunges

  1. Start in a lunge position with one foot forward and the other back, both knees bent at 90 degrees.
  2. Push explosively through both legs to jump into the air.
  3. Switch leg positions mid-air and land softly into a lunge on the opposite side.
  4. Repeat while maintaining control and balance.
