Push-Ups

Strengthen your upper body and core with this classic exercise.

How to Perform

  1. Start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart.
  2. Maintain a straight body line from head to heels.
  3. Lower yourself until your chest nearly touches the floor.
  4. Push through your palms to return to the starting position.

Recommended Routine

Do 3 sets of 12 reps. Take 30–60 seconds rest between sets.

Equipment Needed

  • No equipment needed
  • Optional: Yoga mat for comfort

Benefits

  • Targets chest, shoulders, and triceps
  • Improves core strength
  • Boosts muscular endurance
  • Can be done anywhere, anytime
