💪 Arm Workout

Strengthen and tone your arms with these targeted exercises

Bicep Curls

3 sets of 15 reps using dumbbells. Focus on controlled movements to activate the biceps effectively.

Tricep Dips

3 sets of 12 reps using a bench or sturdy chair. Helps tone and strengthen the triceps.

Overhead Press

3 sets of 12 reps using dumbbells or resistance bands. Great for shoulders and upper arms.

Arm Circles

2 minutes forward and backward. A simple bodyweight movement to warm up and activate the arms.

🏋️ Equipment Needed

  • Dumbbells or resistance bands
  • Sturdy chair or bench (for dips)
  • Comfortable workout clothes

💪 Benefits

  • Strengthens biceps, triceps, and shoulder muscles
  • Improves arm definition and tone
  • Enhances functional upper body strength
  • Supports better posture and joint stability

✅ Tips

  • Focus on slow, controlled movements
  • Keep elbows close to your body for proper form
  • Start with lighter weights and increase gradually
  • Breathe steadily and maintain a tight core
  • Rest 30-60 seconds between sets
