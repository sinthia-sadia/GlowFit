A relaxing yoga stretch that soothes the spine, hips, and mind.

How to Perform

Start on your hands and knees in a tabletop position. Spread your knees wide apart while keeping your big toes touching. Sit your hips back toward your heels and stretch your arms forward. Rest your forehead on the mat. Breathe deeply and hold for 30 seconds to a few minutes.

Benefits

  • Stretches the spine, hips, thighs, and ankles
  • Calms the brain and relieves stress
  • Helps relieve back and neck pain
  • Promotes gentle flexibility and relaxation

Tips for Best Results

  • Use a cushion under your torso for extra comfort
  • Breathe slowly and deeply to relax into the stretch
  • Keep your arms extended or rest them beside your body
  • Hold as long as comfortable, focusing on release
