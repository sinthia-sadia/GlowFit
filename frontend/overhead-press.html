3 sets of 12 reps using dumbbells or resistance bands. Great for shoulders and upper arms.

How to Do Overhead Press

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart holding dumbbells at shoulder height with palms facing forward. Press the weights upward until your arms are fully extended overhead. Slowly lower the dumbbells back to shoulder height.

This exercise strengthens your shoulders and upper arms, helping improve posture and upper body strength.

Benefits of Overhead Press

  • Builds strong and toned shoulders
  • Improves upper body strength and stability
  • Enhances core strength by engaging abs
  • Helps improve posture and balance
  • Increases muscle endurance in the arms

Equipment Needed

  • Dumbbells or resistance bands
  • Flat, stable surface
  • Comfortable workout clothes
